<p><strong>The Side Scan Sonars Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-beam</li><li>Multi-beam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Side scan sonars are employed in underwater surveying and mapping, offering two primary market types: single-beam and multi-beam. Single-beam sonars emit a focused sonar signal in one direction, producing detailed images of the sea floor but with limited coverage. In contrast, multi-beam sonars emit multiple beams simultaneously, providing a wider coverage area and enhanced resolution, capable of creating detailed 3D maps. Each type serves different applications in marine exploration, archaeological surveys, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
