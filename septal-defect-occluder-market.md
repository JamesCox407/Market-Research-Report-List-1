<p><p>The Septal Defect Occluder Market is categorized into two main types: Atrial Septal Defect (ASD) devices and Ventricular Septal Defect (VSD) devices. ASD devices are designed to close openings between the heart's upper chambers, preventing blood shunting and improving oxygenation. In contrast, VSD devices target holes between the heart's lower chambers, addressing issues of unregulated blood flow and heart strain. Both devices are crucial in treating congenital heart defects, enhancing cardiac function, and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
