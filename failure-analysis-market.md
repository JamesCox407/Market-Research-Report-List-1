<p><p>The failure analysis market encompasses various advanced microscopy techniques used to investigate material defects and failures. Scanning Electron Microscopes (SEM) provide high-resolution imaging of surfaces, while Focused Ion Beam (FIB) systems enable precise material removal and imaging at nanoscale levels. Transmission Electron Microscopes (TEM) offer insights into internal structures at atomic resolutions. Dual Beam Systems combine FIB and SEM capabilities, facilitating detailed analysis and sample preparation, enhancing efficiency in identifying and resolving material failures across diverse industries.</p></p>
