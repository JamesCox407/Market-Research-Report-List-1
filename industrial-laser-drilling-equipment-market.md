<p><strong>The Industrial Laser Drilling Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pulsed Fiber Laser</li><li>CW Fiber Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial laser drilling equipment market is primarily segmented into two types: pulsed fiber lasers and continuous wave (CW) fiber lasers. Pulsed fiber lasers emit short bursts of high-intensity laser energy, allowing precise drilling of small holes in various materials, ideal for applications requiring high accuracy. In contrast, CW fiber lasers deliver a continuous beam of laser light, suitable for faster drilling processes with larger hole diameters, making them efficient for high-volume production and thicker materials.</p></p>
