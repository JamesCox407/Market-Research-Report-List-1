<p><p>The low and medium voltage protective relay market is categorized into electromechanical, static, digital, and numerical relays. Electromechanical relays use physical moving parts for operation, offering reliability but slower response times. Static relays rely on solid-state components, providing faster response and greater accuracy. Digital relays combine microprocessor technology with advanced algorithms for enhanced functionality and communication capabilities. Numerical relays, a subset of digital relays, offer even greater precision, allowing for comprehensive monitoring and control in electrical systems.</p></p>
