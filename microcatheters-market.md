<p><p>The microcatheters market is segmented into four main types: </p><p>1. **Delivery Microcatheters** enable targeted drug or embolic agents delivery during procedures.</p><p>2. **Diagnosis Microcatheters** facilitate access for imaging or diagnostic purposes, aiding in disease identification.</p><p>3. **Aspiration Microcatheters** are designed to remove clots or fluids from vessels or cavities, improving patient outcomes.</p><p>4. **Steerable Microcatheters** offer enhanced maneuverability in complex anatomical structures, allowing for precise navigation during interventions. Each type plays a critical role in minimally invasive medical procedures.</p></p>
