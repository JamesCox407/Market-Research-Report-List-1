<p><p>The barge transportation market features distinct types based on cargo requirements. Open barges are flat-deck vessels ideal for bulk goods like coal or gravel, allowing easy loading and unloading. Covered barges are enclosed, providing protection for sensitive materials, such as grains or machinery, from environmental elements during transit. Tank barges, designed for liquid cargo, transport products like chemicals and petroleum safely, equipped with specialized tanks to prevent leaks or spills, ensuring compliance with safety standards.</p></p>
