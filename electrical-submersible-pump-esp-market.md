<p><p>Electrical Submersible Pumps (ESPs) are used to lift fluids from deep underground sources, operating efficiently underwater. The market is primarily segmented into openwell and borewell types. Openwell ESPs are suitable for shallower applications, ideal for agricultural and irrigation needs where water levels are relatively high. In contrast, borewell ESPs are designed for deeper installations, catering to municipal, industrial, and agricultural sectors where water must be extracted from significant depths. Both types enhance fluid extraction capabilities in their respective contexts.</p></p>
