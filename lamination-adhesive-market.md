<p><p>The lamination adhesive market is categorized into three main types: solvent-based, solventless, and water-based adhesives. Solvent-based adhesives contain volatile organic compounds, offering strong bonding but raising environmental concerns. Solventless adhesives, with little to no solvents, provide high-performance bonding and are more environmentally friendly. Water-based adhesives, containing minimal solvents, are safer for indoor use and offer a lower environmental impact, making them popular in various applications. Each type caters to specific needs in the packaging and laminating industries.</p></p>
