<p><ul><li>Primary Brain Tumor</li><li>Secondary Brain Tumor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The brain tumor diagnosis and treatments market is categorized into primary and secondary brain tumors. Primary brain tumors originate within the brain tissue itself, such as gliomas and meningiomas, and typically require specific diagnostic imaging and targeted therapies. Secondary brain tumors, also known as metastatic tumors, arise from cancer cells that spread from other body parts to the brain. The treatment for these tumors often involves a combination of chemotherapy, radiation, and palliative care, tailored to the patient's overall health and cancer progression.</p></p>
