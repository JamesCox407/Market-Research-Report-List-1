<p><p>Textured Soy Protein (TSP) is a meat substitute derived from soybeans, commonly used in vegetarian and vegan dishes. It is favored for its high protein content, low fat, and versatility in culinary applications. TSP is produced by extruding defatted soy flour, resulting in a fibrous structure that mimics the texture of meat. Its nutritional profile makes it a popular choice among health-conscious consumers and those seeking plant-based diets.</p><p>The Textured Soy Protein Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.8% during the forecast period, driven by increasing consumer awareness of the benefits of plant-based diets. Factors such as rising vegetarianism, flexitarian lifestyles, and the demand for sustainable protein sources contribute to this growth. Health trends emphasizing high-protein, low-carb diets also bolster the market. </p><p>In addition, innovations in product formulations and expanded applications in processed foods, snacks, and ready-to-eat meals are shaping trends. The rising availability of textured soy protein in various forms, such as granules and flakes, further enhances its appeal. As consumers prioritize sustainability and health, the textured soy protein market is poised for significant expansion, catering to diverse dietary preferences and evolving consumer needs.</p></p>
