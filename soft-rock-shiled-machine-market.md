<p><strong>The Soft-Rock Shiled Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Slurry Shield (SS)</li><li>Earth Pressure Balance Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Soft-Rock Shield Machine market includes two primary types: Slurry Shield (SS) and Earth Pressure Balance Machines (EPB). Slurry Shield machines are designed for tunneling in soft, saturated soils, utilizing a bentonite-based slurry to support the excavation face and manage groundwater. In contrast, Earth Pressure Balance machines are utilized in various soil conditions, balancing the earth pressure during excavation, which minimizes ground settlement. Both types are essential for efficient underground construction projects in soft rock environments.</p></p>
