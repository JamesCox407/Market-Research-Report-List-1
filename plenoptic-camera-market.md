<p><p>The Plenoptic Camera market is primarily divided into two types: Standard Plenoptic Cameras and Focused Plenoptic Cameras. Standard Plenoptic Cameras capture light fields without adjusting focus, allowing for post-capture focus adjustments and depth information retrieval. In contrast, Focused Plenoptic Cameras optimize focus at the time of capture, enhancing image clarity at specific depths. Both types leverage unique light-field imaging technologies, catering to various applications in photography, film, and scientific research, where depth perception and flexibility in focus are essential.</p></p>
