<p><strong>The Medical Housings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vacuum Forming</li><li>Pressure Forming</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical housings market encompasses two primary types: vacuum forming and pressure forming. Vacuum forming involves heating a thermoplastic sheet until pliable, then forming it over a mold using a vacuum to create precise and lightweight structures ideal for medical devices. Conversely, pressure forming uses both air pressure and mechanical force, enabling more complex geometries and finer details. Both methods are essential for producing durable and hygienic housings for various medical applications, enhancing functionality and patient safety.</p></p>
