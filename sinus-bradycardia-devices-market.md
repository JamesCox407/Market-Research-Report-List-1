<p><p>The Sinus Bradycardia Devices market primarily includes pacemakers and implantable cardioverter defibrillators (ICDs). Pacemakers are employed to regulate slow heart rates by delivering electrical impulses, ensuring the heart beats effectively. Meanwhile, ICDs are designed to monitor heart rhythms and deliver shocks to correct life-threatening arrhythmias, including extremely slow heart rates. Together, these devices address the needs of patients with bradycardia and other heart rhythm disorders, offering crucial support for maintaining adequate cardiac function and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
