<p><strong>The Intraocular Lens (IOLs) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Non-foldable lenses</li><li>Foldable Intraocular Lens</li><li>Hydrophilic</li><li>Hydrophobic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Intraocular Lens (IOL) market features various types tailored for cataract surgery. Non-foldable lenses, typically made from rigid materials, are implanted through larger incisions and are less commonly used today. Foldable IOLs, made from flexible materials, allow for smaller incisions and quicker recovery. Within foldable lenses, hydrophilic IOLs absorb water, increasing biocompatibility, while hydrophobic lenses repel water, enhancing surface properties and reducing complications. Together, these innovations enhance patient outcomes and drive market growth.</p></p>
