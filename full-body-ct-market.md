<p><strong>The Full-body CT Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CT Scanners</li><li>PET-CT Scanners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The full-body CT market comprises two main types: CT scanners and PET-CT scanners. CT scanners utilize X-ray technology to produce detailed cross-sectional images of the body, useful for diagnosing various medical conditions. PET-CT scanners, on the other hand, combine positron emission tomography (PET) with computed tomography (CT) to assess metabolic activity and anatomical structure simultaneously, enhancing diagnostic accuracy, particularly in oncology. Both types are instrumental in improving patient outcomes through early detection and precise treatment planning.</p></p>
