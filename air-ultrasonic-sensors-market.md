<p><p>The air ultrasonic sensors market is divided into high-frequency and low-frequency sensor types. High-frequency sensors operate at frequencies typically above 100 kHz and are ideal for applications requiring precision and sensitivity, such as object detection and distance measurement. In contrast, low-frequency sensors, operating below 100 kHz, are better suited for applications involving larger objects or longer distances, offering a wider detection range but with less sensitivity. Both types serve critical roles in industrial, automotive, and consumer applications.</p></p>
