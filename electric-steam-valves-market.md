<p><ul><li>Single Acting Steam Valve</li><li>Double Acting Steam Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric steam valves market is categorized into single acting and double acting steam valves. Single acting steam valves operate using a spring return mechanism, requiring minimal electrical power to control the flow of steam, making them cost-effective and simple. In contrast, double acting steam valves utilize electric power to both open and close the valve, offering more precise control and faster operation. This versatility caters to diverse industrial applications, enhancing efficiency and reliability in steam management systems.</p></p>
