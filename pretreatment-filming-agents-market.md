<p><p>The pretreatment filming agents market comprises three main types: inorganic anti-corrosion agents, organic anti-corrosion agents, and polymer anti-corrosion agents. Inorganic agents, like phosphates and chromates, provide robust protection against corrosion through conversion coatings. Organic agents, including fatty acids and amines, form protective films on metal surfaces to prevent rust. Polymer anti-corrosion agents utilize synthetic materials to create barriers against moisture and corrosive elements, offering durability and flexibility. Each type serves specific applications in industrial and automotive sectors.</p></p>
