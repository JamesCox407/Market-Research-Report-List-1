<p><p>Surgical lighting systems include LED and halogen surgical lamps, each offering distinct advantages. LED surgical lamps utilize advanced technology, providing bright, focused light with adjustable intensity and color temperature, enhancing visibility during procedures while minimizing heat output. They are energy-efficient and have a longer lifespan. In contrast, halogen surgical lamps produce warm, consistent illumination and are generally more affordable, but they generate more heat and have a shorter operational life. Both types are essential in ensuring optimal surgical conditions.</p></p>
