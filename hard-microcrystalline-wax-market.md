<p><p>The Hard Microcrystalline Wax market can be categorized into two main types based on melting points: low melting point and high melting point waxes. Low melting point microcrystalline waxes typically range from 60°C to 70°C, offering flexibility and excellent adhesion, making them suitable for applications like candles, cosmetics, and food packaging. High melting point waxes, ranging from 70°C to 100°C, provide enhanced thermal stability and rigidity, which are ideal for uses in industrial formulations, coatings, and specialized lubricants.</p></p>
