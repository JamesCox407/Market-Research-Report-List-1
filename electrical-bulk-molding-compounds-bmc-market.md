The market is generally divided into two main types: Polyester Base and Vinyl Ester Base. Polyester Base BMC is known for its excellent mechanical properties and cost-effectiveness, making it suitable for general-purpose electrical components. In contrast, Vinyl Ester Base BMC offers enhanced chemical resistance and thermal stability, making it ideal for demanding environments, such as in electrical housings and circuit boards.</p></p>
