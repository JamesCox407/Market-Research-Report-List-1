<p><p>The CAE market encompasses various analytical domains crucial for design and engineering. Finite Element Analysis (FEA) assesses structural integrity by simulating stresses and strains. Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD) studies fluid flow behavior, essential for optimizing designs in aerodynamics and heat transfer. Multibody Dynamics analyzes the interaction between interconnected bodies in motion, useful in mechanical system simulations. Optimization techniques focus on enhancing performance and efficiency, while simulation enables virtual testing of designs under real-world conditions, streamlining development processes.</p></p>
