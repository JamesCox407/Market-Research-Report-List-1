<p><strong>The Gynecology Electrosurgical Units (ESUs) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monopole Electrosurgical Units</li><li>Bipolar Electrosurgical Units</li><li>Vessel Sealing Units</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The gynecology electrosurgical units (ESUs) market comprises three main types: monopole electrosurgical units, bipolar electrosurgical units, and vessel sealing units. Monopole ESUs utilize a single probe for cutting and coagulation, suitable for various gynecological procedures. Bipolar ESUs employ two electrodes, offering precise control and minimized thermal spread, ideal for delicate tissue work. Vessel sealing units focus on sealing blood vessels with minimal damage to surrounding tissues, enhancing safety and efficiency in surgical operations. Each type serves specific surgical needs in gynecology.</p></p>
