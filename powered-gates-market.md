<p><strong>The Powered Gates Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Switch</li><li>ECU</li><li>Latch</li><li>Motor/Actuator</li><li>Relay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Powered gates market encompasses several key components: </p><p>1. **Switch**: Devices that control gate movement by sending signals to the operational system. </p><p>2. **ECU (Electronic Control Unit)**: Manages gate functions, integrating sensors and automation for seamless operation. </p><p>3. **Latch**: Ensures the gate remains securely closed when not in use. </p><p>4. **Motor/Actuator**: Drives the gate's movement, either opening or closing it as required. </p><p>5. **Relay**: Acts as a switch to operate the motor, enhancing circuit control and safety. </p><p>Together, these components ensure the efficiency and security of powered gate systems.</p></p>
