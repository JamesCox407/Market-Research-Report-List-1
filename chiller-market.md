<p><strong>The Chiller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Screw Chiller</li><li>Scroll Chiller</li><li>Centrifugal Chiller</li><li>Absorption Chiller</li><li>Reciprocating Chiller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The chiller market encompasses various types designed for different cooling applications. Screw chillers use twin-screw compressors for high efficiency and reliability in large-scale cooling. Scroll chillers employ spiral elements for compact, quiet operation, ideal for smaller spaces. Centrifugal chillers utilize rotating impellers, best suited for large cooling loads. Absorption chillers use heat sources like natural gas for cooling, making them energy-efficient. Reciprocating chillers feature piston-driven compressors, suitable for low to medium capacity loads, offering versatility in industrial applications.</p></p>
