<p><p>The laparoscope market is divided into two main types: rigid and flexible laparoscopes. Rigid laparoscopes are commonly used in traditional minimally invasive surgeries, featuring a straight design that offers excellent visualization and precision in procedures. In contrast, flexible laparoscopes provide increased maneuverability and adaptability, allowing surgeons to navigate complex anatomical structures and perform intricate operations. Both types enhance surgical outcomes by minimizing patient trauma and recovery time while enabling greater access to targeted areas within the body.</p></p>
