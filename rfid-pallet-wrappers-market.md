<p><strong>The RFID Pallet Wrappers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultra-High Frequency</li><li>High Frequency and Near Field Communication</li><li>Low Frequency</li><li>Dual Frequency</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RFID pallet wrappers utilize various frequency types for tracking and managing pallets. Ultra-High Frequency (UHF) provides long-range reading capabilities, ideal for efficient inventory management. High Frequency (HF) is commonly used for applications requiring short-range communication, suitable for close-proximity scanning. Near Field Communication (NFC) enables interactions at very short distances, often used in consumer-oriented applications. Low Frequency (LF) offers reliability in challenging environments but with shorter read ranges. Dual Frequency combines benefits of multiple frequencies, enhancing flexibility and performance in diverse settings.</p></p>
