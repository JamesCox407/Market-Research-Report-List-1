<p><p>The diabetes drug market encompasses various treatment types, including insulin, which helps manage blood sugar levels by facilitating glucose uptake. Sensitizers enhance the body’s sensitivity to insulin, while SGLT-2 inhibitors reduce glucose reabsorption in the kidneys. Alpha-glucosidase inhibitors slow carbohydrate absorption in the intestines, and secretagogues stimulate insulin secretion from the pancreas. Peptide analogs mimic hormones involved in glucose regulation. The "others" category includes less common medications, addressing diverse treatment needs within diabetes management.</p></p>
