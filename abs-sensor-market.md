<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ABS sensor market comprises two primary types: Hall Effect sensors and Magnetic Inductive sensors. Hall Effect sensors utilize a semiconducting material to detect changes in the magnetic field, providing precise wheel speed data essential for Anti-lock Braking Systems (ABS). In contrast, Magnetic Inductive sensors rely on the generation of an electrical signal from a magnetic field interacting with a moving magnet. Both types play crucial roles in enhancing vehicle safety and performance by preventing wheel lock during braking.</p></p>
