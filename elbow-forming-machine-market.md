<p><strong>The Elbow Forming Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Elbow Cold Forming Machine</li><li>Elbow Hot Forming Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The elbow forming machine market is divided into two main types: elbow cold forming machines and elbow hot forming machines. Elbow cold forming machines operate at room temperature, using pressure to shape metal into elbows, making them suitable for precise applications requiring high dimensional accuracy. In contrast, elbow hot forming machines utilize elevated temperatures to deform metal, allowing for complex shapes and reducing material stress. Both types are essential in industries like plumbing, HVAC, and oil and gas for efficient production of piping components.</p></p>
