<p><p>The semi-axial flow pump market is divided into two main types: vertical and horizontal semi-axial flow pumps. Vertical semi-axial flow pumps are designed with a vertical orientation, ideal for applications requiring space efficiency and effective liquid handling in confined areas. Horizontal semi-axial flow pumps, on the other hand, are laid out horizontally, which allows for easier maintenance and installation. Both designs optimize flow characteristics, making them suitable for irrigation, drainage, and industrial processes that demand reliable water movement.</p></p>
