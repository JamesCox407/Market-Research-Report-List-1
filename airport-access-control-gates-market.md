<p><p>The Airport Access Control Gates market is primarily divided into two types: Swing Type and Sliding Type. Swing Type gates operate by pivoting on hinges, allowing for wider openings and a more traditional design, often used in secure areas requiring high traffic flow. Sliding Type gates, on the other hand, open horizontally along a track, providing a space-saving solution ideal for narrower spaces and maintaining aesthetic appeal. Both types enhance security and manage passenger flow effectively in airport environments.</p></p>
