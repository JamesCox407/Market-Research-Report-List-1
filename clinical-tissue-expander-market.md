<p><p>Clinical tissue expanders are medical devices used in reconstructive surgery to create additional skin or tissue. They are typically inserted beneath the skin or muscle to promote gradual tissue growth over time, allowing for reconstruction in cases of trauma, cancer surgery, or congenital defects. The expanders are gradually filled with saline, leading to tissue stretching and preparation for subsequent surgical procedures such as implant placement.</p><p>The Clinical Tissue Expander Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 3.40% during the forecast period, driven by increasing awareness regarding reconstructive surgeries and advancements in medical technology.
