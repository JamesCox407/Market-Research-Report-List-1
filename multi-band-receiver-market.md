In contrast, Multichannel receivers can process multiple bands concurrently, offering greater flexibility and enhanced capabilities for complex communications and signal analysis. This segmentation allows users to choose based on their specific needs, from simpler dual-band setups to more sophisticated multichannel configurations that support diverse applications.</p></p>
