<p><strong>The Medical Respiratory Tract Humidifier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Type</li><li>Passive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Medical Respiratory Tract Humidifier Market is categorized into two main types: Active and Passive. Active humidifiers use heated water to create steam, delivering warm, moisture-rich air directly to the patient, enhancing comfort and treatment efficacy. In contrast, Passive humidifiers utilize ambient air to passively warm and humidify the respiratory gases, typically relying on the patient's own body heat and moisture. Both types aim to prevent airway irritation, improve respiratory function, and enhance overall patient outcomes.</p></p>
