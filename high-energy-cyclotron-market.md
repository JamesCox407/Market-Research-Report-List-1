<p><p>The high energy cyclotron market comprises two main types: single particle cyclotrons and multi-particle cyclotrons. Single particle cyclotrons are designed to accelerate a single type of particle, often protons, used primarily in medical applications like cancer treatment and imaging. In contrast, multi-particle cyclotrons can accelerate various particles, including ions, making them suitable for a wider range of applications, such as materials research and fundamental physics experiments. Both play crucial roles in advancing nuclear medicine and scientific research.</p></p>
