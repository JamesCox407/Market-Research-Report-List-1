<p><p>A Powder X-ray Diffractometer (PXRD) is an analytical instrument used primarily for the characterization of crystalline materials. It works by directing X-ray beams onto the sample powder, which diffract the X-rays at specific angles, providing a unique diffraction pattern that reveals information about the material's phase, crystallography, and structural properties. PXRD is widely utilized in materials science, chemistry, and pharmaceuticals for quality control and research purposes.</p><p>The PXRD market is demonstrating significant growth, driven by increasing demand in various industries, including pharmaceuticals, materials science, and nanotechnology.
