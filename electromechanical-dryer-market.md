<p><ul><li>Electro Dryer</li><li>Mechanical Dryer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electromechanical dryer market comprises two main types: electro dryers and mechanical dryers. Electro dryers employ electrical energy to facilitate moisture removal through heating elements or resistance methods, optimizing efficiency and reducing drying time. Meanwhile, mechanical dryers utilize mechanical processes, such as tumbling or centrifugal force, to enhance airflow and moisture extraction. Both types address various industrial and commercial drying needs, catering to sectors like food processing, textiles, and pharmaceuticals, while emphasizing energy efficiency and effective moisture management.</p></p>
