<p><ul><li>Polymer Laminate EC</li><li>All-solid-state EC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electrochromic (EC) windows market features two primary types: polymer laminate EC and all-solid-state EC. Polymer laminate EC windows utilize a flexible layer of electrochromic materials sandwiched between transparent conductive films, allowing for dynamic tinting and energy efficiency. In contrast, all-solid-state EC windows consist of a robust, rigid design using inorganic materials that enhance durability and performance. Both types serve to regulate glare and heat, improving indoor comfort and energy savings in residential and commercial buildings.</p></p>
