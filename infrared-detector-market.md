<p><strong>The Infrared Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Uncooled Infrared Detector</li><li>Cooled Infrared Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared detector market is primarily divided into two types: uncooled and cooled infrared detectors. Uncooled detectors, typically based on microbolometer technology, operate at ambient temperatures, making them cost-effective and suitable for a wide range of applications, including thermal imaging and automotive sensing. In contrast, cooled infrared detectors utilize cryogenic cooling to enhance sensitivity and are ideal for high-performance applications such as military surveillance and astrophysical observations, where detecting faint thermal signatures is crucial.</p></p>
