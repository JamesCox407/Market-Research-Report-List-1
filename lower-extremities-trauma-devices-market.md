<p><p>The lower extremities trauma devices market comprises several key types used to stabilize fractures and support orthopedic healing. Internal fixators involve devices implanted within the body to realign bones, offering strong support. Plates and screws secure fractured bones by directly attaching them, promoting stability during recovery. Rods and pins are utilized for intramedullary fixation, providing internal support through the bone shaft. Additionally, "others" encompass various emerging technologies and adjunct devices that aid in the treatment and stabilization of lower extremity injuries.</p></p>
