<p><strong>The Passive Dosimeters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optically Stimulated Luminescence (OSL) Dosimeters</li><li>Thermoluminescent Dosimeters (TLD)</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The passive dosimeter market includes various types that measure radiation exposure without requiring active data collection. Optically Stimulated Luminescence (OSL) dosimeters utilize light stimulation to read stored energy from a crystal, providing accurate dose measurements. Thermoluminescent Dosimeters (TLD) rely on heating crystals to release stored energy as light, revealing radiation exposure levels. Other market types may include films or chemical detectors that also measure radiation through different passive mechanisms, catering to diverse monitoring needs across industries.</p></p>
