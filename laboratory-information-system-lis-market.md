<p><p>A Laboratory Information System (LIS) is software that manages laboratory processes, including data management, workflow automation, and reporting of test results. The market for LIS is divided into two main types: on-premises LIS and cloud-based LIS. On-premises LIS involves local installation on a laboratory's servers, offering control and customization but requiring significant IT resources. In contrast, cloud-based LIS is hosted on remote servers, providing scalability, reduced IT burden, and easier access, making it ideal for modern, flexible laboratory environments.</p></p>
