<p><p>The stainless steel safety valve market is categorized into hydraulic and pneumatic safety valves, each serving distinct applications. Hydraulic safety valves are designed to manage pressure in fluid systems, ensuring safe operation by releasing excess pressure when necessary. In contrast, pneumatic safety valves regulate gas pressure, preventing over-pressurization in pneumatic systems. Both types are vital in various industries, including oil and gas, chemical, and manufacturing, where safety and equipment protection are paramount. Their durability and corrosion resistance make stainless steel an ideal material.</p></p>
