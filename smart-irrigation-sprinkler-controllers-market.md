<p><p>The smart irrigation sprinkler controllers market consists of two primary types: weather-based controllers and sensor-based controllers. Weather-based controllers utilize real-time weather data to adjust watering schedules, optimizing water usage based on precipitation forecasts and temperature. In contrast, sensor-based controllers employ soil moisture sensors to monitor the water levels in the ground and automatically adjust irrigation based on actual needs. Both types enhance water efficiency, reduce waste, and promote sustainable landscaping practices in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
