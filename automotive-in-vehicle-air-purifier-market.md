<p><p>The automotive in-vehicle air purifier market comprises three main types: air purifiers, air ionizers, and hybrid systems. Air purifiers utilize filters to capture dust, pollen, and pollutants, enhancing air quality within vehicles. Air ionizers release negatively charged ions to neutralize airborne particles, reducing odors and allergens. Hybrid systems combine both technologies, offering comprehensive air purification by filtering contaminants and ionizing the air for improved overall cabin environment, ensuring a healthier driving experience for passengers.</p></p>
