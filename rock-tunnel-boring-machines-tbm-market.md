<p><p>Rock Tunnel Boring Machines (TBMs) are specialized equipment used for excavating tunnels through various geological conditions. The market encompasses different types, notably open-type TBMs and shielded hard rock TBMs. Open-type TBMs are ideal for soft ground conditions and allow for efficient excavation, featuring an open face design. In contrast, shielded hard rock TBMs are designed for more challenging terrains, equipped with a protective shield to support tunnel stability while boring through hard rock, ensuring safety and efficiency in tunnel construction.</p></p>
