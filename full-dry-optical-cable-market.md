<p><strong>The Full Dry Optical Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Skeleton</li><li>Stranded</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Full Dry Optical Cable market is categorized primarily into two types: Skeleton and Stranded. Skeleton cables feature tightly bound fiber optics alongside minimal reinforcement, optimizing flexibility and lightweight design, suitable for aerial or tight spaces. In contrast, Stranded cables involve fibers arranged in a loose, spiral formation, providing enhanced protection against mechanical stress and environmental factors. Both types are designed for outdoor applications, ensuring reliable performance in varied conditions while catering to different installation requirements and operational demands.</p></p>
