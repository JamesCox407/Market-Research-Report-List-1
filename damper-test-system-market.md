<p><ul><li>Magnetorheological Damper</li><li>Hydraulic Damper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The damper test system market features two primary types: magnetorheological dampers and hydraulic dampers. Magnetorheological dampers use magnetic fields to control fluid viscosity, providing adaptive damping characteristics useful in automotive and industrial applications. In contrast, hydraulic dampers function by dissipating energy through fluid movement, offering traditional damping solutions in various applications, including machinery and vehicles. Both types are essential for enhancing performance, safety, and comfort in systems requiring precise motion control and stability management.</p></p>
