<p><p>The polypropylene filtration fabric market is primarily divided into two types: polypropylene monofilament filtration fabric and polypropylene multifilament filtration fabric. Monofilament fabric is made from single continuous strands, offering high strength and superior filtration efficiency, ideal for applications needing precise particle retention. In contrast, multifilament fabric consists of multiple intertwined strands, providing flexibility and increased permeability, making it suitable for diverse filtration processes. Both types are widely used across industries such as water treatment, food processing, and industrial filtration.</p></p>
